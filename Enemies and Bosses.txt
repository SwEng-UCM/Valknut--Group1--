Basic Enemies

	Ice Giant:

		Attack --> The giant swings his club in your direction

		Elemental attack --> The monster uses its magic to call upon a blizzard to freeze you

		Element --> Ice

	Fire Giant:

		Attack --> The flaming monster charges at you with its sword

		Elemental attack --> The giant uses his fiery breath and unleashes a fire torrent angainst you

		Element --> Fire

	Animal Shapeshifter:

		Attack --> In his animal form the shapeshifter slashes you with its claws

		Elemental attack --> Using all of their power the shapeshifter tranforms into a giant bear to maul you

		Element --> Chaos

	Berserker:

		Attack --> The warrior launches a barrage of punches and axe slashes

		Elemental attack --> The berserker drinks its potion and lunges torwards you with amplified strength and might 

		Element --> Blood

	Elf:

		Attack --> Chanels the magic of the runes to harm you

		Elemental attack --> The elf uses the rune of Frey to manipulate the sorrounding nature and make it attack you

		Element --> Nature

Bosses

	Fisrt Boss: Thrym. Thrym was the giant who stole Thor's hammer. He is the king of ice giants.

		Special mechanic --> Can change attacks depending on the weapon he uses (more damage to individual / hits all players at once) 

		Attack --> Thrym attacks you with his gigantic axe, leaving a huge hole on the floor under you

		Special attack 1 (multi hit) --> Thrym throws Mjolnir at you, and it ricochets between you and your partners

		Special attack 2 (ignores defend) --> Thrym commands Summarbrander and makes it slash you

		Special attack 3 (always hits) --> Thrym throws Gungnir torwards you, and it doesn't stop until it reaches you

		Element --> Ice

	Second Boss: Fafnir. A dwarf turned into a fiery dragon.
